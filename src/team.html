Create Your Own Team

Community service can be done by individuals or as a group. You can create and manage your local team.

This is the crucial aspect of the program. There are recruitment sheets for individuals who want to form groups. There is also a community form that tracks all your community service.
In this form, you record the places where you serve. A reputable person from the organization you serve in acts as your supervisor and he/she has to sign the number of hours of service you perform.
This sheet will be submitted at the end of the month for validation.

What is the aim of 20/30 challenge 2018 edition?

Our primary aim is to highlight the need and importance of community service in our society and to get 100 youth participating in the program all over Nigeria and perhaps the world. The secondary aim is to make this one of the biggest and most trending events on social media in 2018.

What is expected of our Volunteers?

To be good ambassadors in all the places they serve. We also expect that the community forms are properly filled for validation at the end of the month. We are also hoping that our volunteers would spread the word and recruit other persons so that we can reach our target of 100 volunteers.
